Overview

The Commonwealth of Vlagzor is the 194th independent country recognized by the United Nations. It is located in Northwestern europe, 120 miles off the coast of Ireland. It is a Multi-Party Constitutional Parlimentary-Monarchy. A former territory of Denmark, it has won independence through the election of 1978.

Info

The Capital of Vlagzor is Ambleside. The official religion of Vlagzor is Catholicism. The official languages of Vlagzor are Vlagzorian Danish, Danish, and English. The Vlagzorian GDP is about 1.7 trillion Unites states dollars, with a GDP per Capita of $33,000. The official Currency is the Vlagzorian Krone (VKRO). It mainly exports petroleum to the european union, through a company known as NordFlow.
