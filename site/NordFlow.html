Overview

NordFlow is a Government-Ran Company producing Oil and Natural Gas. It operates all oil rigs in Vlagzor and is the only company permitted to extract Vlagzor’s rich oil reserves. It’s first pipeline, Nord 1, was completed on the 9th of January 2026, with oil beginning to flow on the 12th of January, which is predicted to either curb or fully eliminate Europe’s dependence on Russian oil.
